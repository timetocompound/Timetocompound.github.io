Tomorrow's Federal Reserve (FED) announcement is eagerly anticipated by global markets. The rate decision will not only determine the short-term pace of the US economy but also the trajectory of the S&P 500 and Bitcoin.

What we're looking for: Markets have already priced in a rate pause. The real movement will come from Powell's "dot plot" (guidance) and when the first rate cut is expected.

1. The 3 Potential Scenarios

  1. **"Hawkish Pause" (Most Likely):** Rates maintained, but with a statement that they "will remain high for longer."
  2. **"Dovish Pause":** Rates maintained, with softer language and acknowledgment of inflation control. (Positive for stocks).
  3. **Unexpected Hike:** Highly unlikely, but if it happens, it will trigger a sharp sell-off across all markets.

2. Impact on the S&P 500

The stock market hates uncertainty. If Powell provides clarity for 2024 (even if it's "tough"), markets will breathe a sigh of relief. The key is the guidance on corporate earnings outlook.

3. Bitcoin and Interest Rates

Bitcoin now behaves as a risk asset. If rates remain high, the cost of borrowing increases, and investment capital moves to safer assets (like bonds). A "dovish" turn would be very positive for crypto.

Conclusion

Everyone is waiting for the pivot, but the FED is likely to remain cautious. Be prepared for volatility tomorrow afternoon, regardless of the final decision.
