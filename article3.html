Albert Einstein is often quoted as saying, "Compound interest is the eighth wonder of the world. He who understands it, earns it... he who doesn't, pays it."

At the heart of long-term investment success lies the power of compounding.

Compounding: This is the phenomenon where the earnings you generate from your investment start generating earnings themselves. You earn money on your earnings.

2. The Time Factor

The longer the time period, the more exponential the growth becomes. In the early years, the difference is small. However, after the 10th or 15th year, the curve begins to climb dramatically.

This means that time is your greatest ally, even if you start with small amounts.

3. How to Leverage it

  1. Start Early: The greatest power of compounding is time. Don't be afraid of small amounts.
  2. Reinvest: Do not withdraw profits. Leave them in the investment to generate more profits.
  3. Consistency: Continue adding money to the investment regularly (e.g., monthly).

Conclusion

Compound interest is not magic, it is math, and it works silently in the background, building your wealth. Learn to use it to your advantage.
